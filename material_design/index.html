    About Me

    I am a junior who wants to apply my knowledge of Computer Engineering principles and gain valuable experience through co-op employment.

    I love creating things. My love for electronic music led me to start creating my own music while in high school. These musical experiments made me curious to explore the field of Computer Engineering. My journey in this field thus far inspires me to help solve real-world problems through the application of technology and music.
